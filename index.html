OUR STORY

Sushi Maru invites you to experience a kaiten dining serving both traditional and creative sushi in a relaxed and friendly atmosphere. Our extensive menu features a wide array of sushi, and specialty as well as tataki, udon, cooked appetizers and desserts for everyone.

Sushi Maru will always strive to create an exceptional dining experience by providing the highest quality available while being affordable with professional and attentive service for our guests.

SERVICES

Corporate Lunch Catering - Sushi Maru Bellevue specializes in corporate events from board room luncheons to formal retirement parties. We have the right menu for your needs and the experience to bring you the best.

Event Catering - Sushi Maru Bellevue welcomes the opportunity to help you plan your next event catering. Private Parties, Company Banquets or Other Parties. In order to ensure the most efficient service, please follow these procedures when making your arrangements.

Successful events begin with proper and prior planning. Please be sure to allow enough time so that you will receive exactly what you need. Events should be planned as early as possible.

In the case of an urgent event or last minute request, contact us immediately. We will begin to help you in anyway possible.

Beer and wine service is available.

We can create a special menu for any event. Please contact us as soon as possible to expedite the planning process.
